Our Story

Where Stories Unfold Over Great Food

At 0125 Cafe Mystic Falls, we believe every meal is a story waiting to be told. Nestled in the heart of Nanded, our cafe has been a beloved destination for food lovers, students, and families for years.

What sets us apart? Our huge, spacious interiors that never feel crowded, combined with an extensive menu that spans from authentic Chinese delicacies to handcrafted pizzas and refreshing mocktails. Whether you're craving our signature Tandoori Paneer Pizza or our legendary Brownie Shake, every dish is crafted with care and served with a smile.

From college students celebrating milestones to families enjoying quality time together, we've hosted countless memories within our walls. Our VIP suite offers the perfect setting for your special celebrations, while our affordable pricing ensures everyone can enjoy premium taste without breaking the bank.

Great food, warm ambience, and memories that last forever.
